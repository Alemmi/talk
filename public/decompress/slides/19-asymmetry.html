Key insight The race between F6 and S6 is not “two syscalls fired at the same instant.” It's “two pipelines where one has one extra I/O hop before its critical syscall.”

Therefore by the time the file chain even submits its open(2), the symlink chain has already completed its symlink(2). The check on F4 is pointless — the symlink lands in the gap between the check and the use.
