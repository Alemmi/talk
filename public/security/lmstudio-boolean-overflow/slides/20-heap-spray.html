Heap spray — true by saturation

the problem the spray solves
  • pimpl now points at 0x100000001 — but nothing is there yet
  • Non-PIE heap starts low and fixed, so we can grow allocations up to it
  • Cover the ~4 GB range with our bytes — make the address real

The outcome After the spray, 0x100000001 — and the cells at fixed offsets around it — hold bytes we chose. The corrupted pimpl finally points at memory we control.

What we put there The pointers the destructor will read, and the argv / /bin/sh for the final execvp — we fill those in as we reach each step.
